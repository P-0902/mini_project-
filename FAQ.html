Frequently Asked Questions

What is 'Journey to Code'?

'Journey to Code' is a platform designed to help users learn coding through interactive lessons and exercises.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions provided.

Can I access the platform on mobile?

Yes, 'Journey to Code' is fully responsive and can be accessed on any device, including mobile phones.

Is there a way to track my progress?

Yes, your progress is saved automatically in your profile, and you can view it on the dashboard.

Do I need prior coding knowledge to start?

No, 'Journey to Code' is beginner-friendly and provides step-by-step guidance for all levels.
